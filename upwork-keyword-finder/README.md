# 🔍 Upwork Keyword Finder

**Supercharge your Upwork proposal strategy with AI-driven keyword insights.**

Upwork Keyword Finder is a powerful Chrome extension designed for freelancers who want to win more jobs by using the exact language clients are looking for. It automatically scans Upwork job postings and extracts the most important skills, job titles, and action phrases using a combination of smart statistical algorithms and cutting-edge AI.

---

## ✨ Key Features

*   **🧠 Dual-Engine Analysis**: Get the best of both worlds. Our "Smart Algorithm" finds the most frequent keywords statistically, while our "Deep AI" understands the context to suggest hidden gems.
*   **📊 Interactive Side Panel**: View all your insights in a clean, easy-to-use dashboard that stays open while you browse Upwork.
*   **✨ Auto-Highlighting**: The extension automatically highlights the most relevant keywords directly on the Upwork page, helping you spot key requirements instantly.
*   **📈 Success Analytics**: Track your scraping history and see how job trends change over time with built-in charts.
*   **🚀 Blazing Fast Performance**: Features an intelligent caching system that remembers previously analyzed jobs, making subsequent runs near-instant.
*   **📋 One-Click Export**: Easily copy all extracted keywords to your clipboard for use in your proposals or AI cover letter generators.

## 🛠️ How to Install

As this is a specialized tool for professional freelancers, you can install it manually in just a few steps:

1.  **Download the Extension**: Download the project folder to your computer.
2.  **Open Chrome Extensions**: In your Chrome browser, go to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  **Enable Developer Mode**: Toggle the **"Developer mode"** switch in the top right corner.
4.  **Load the Extension**: Click the **"Load unpacked"** button and select the `upwork-keyword-finder` folder you downloaded.
5.  **Pin for Easy Access**: Click the puzzle icon in your Chrome toolbar and "Pin" the Upwork Keyword Finder.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

Using the tool is simple and fits perfectly into your workflow:

1.  **Search for Jobs**: Go to Upwork and perform a job search (e.g., "React Developer").
2.  **Open the Tool**: Click the extension icon to open the side panel.
3.  **Scrape Jobs**: Click **"Scrape Jobs on This Page"** on the Home tab. The tool will read the jobs currently visible on your screen.
4.  **Analyze**: Switch to the **"Keyword Finder"** tab and click **"Analyze Keywords"**. 
5.  **View Results**:
    *   **Skills**: See the most requested technologies and soft skills.
    *   **Titles**: Discover what specific job titles clients are using.
    *   **Phrases**: Find common phrases like "long term" or "expert level" to tailor your tone.
6.  **Write Better Proposals**: Use these keywords in your cover letter to rank higher in client searches and show you truly understand the project.

## 🔒 Privacy & Safety

*   **Local Processing**: All job data is stored locally on your computer. We do not send your data to any external servers (except for the AI model processing which happens securely via Google's Chrome AI).
*   **Safe for Upwork**: The extension mimics human reading behavior and does not interact with Upwork's internal APIs, keeping your account safe.

## 📝 Requirements

*   **Google Chrome Browser** (Version 127 or higher recommended).
*   **Built-in AI**: For the best experience, ensure your Chrome has "Gemini Nano" enabled (Settings > Privacy and security > Ads privacy > AI features).
